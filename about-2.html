About Us

Mancheswar International School, your child’s second home has been Shaping Minds and Touching lives. We provide our students with a safe learning environment that helps them grow as unique personalities. We know that each child is different and hence a one size fits all teaching method would never do. We employ several innovative teaching techniques that impart knowledge and instill strong values into the child to become responsible future citizens of the world. Along with traditional lessons, we encourage our students to open their minds to a myriadof extracurricular activities, from Music and Dance to swimming and games.
